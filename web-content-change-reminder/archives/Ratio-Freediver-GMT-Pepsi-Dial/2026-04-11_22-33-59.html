    Two Time Zones, One Glance

    Every second counts in the pursuit of adventure, and the Freediver GMT is your steadfast companion across the globe. With the GMT hand seamlessly aligning your time across zones, this watch ensures that no moment of your expedition is lost.

    Parallel Timekeeping

    Beneath the watch’s crisp white dial lies more than just time—it’s your gateway to epic exploration. Equipped with a powerhouse Japanese automatic movement, the 24-hour markers are designed for clarity, and with the sapphire crystal protecting the dial from harsh elements, the Freediver stands unyielding. There’s the Ratio logo at 12, and the date window at 3 o'clock makes sure you never miss a step as you plunge deeper into your next adventure.

    Resilient Design

    This is a watch made to endure. The 316L stainless steel case and crown are crafted for resilience, built to withstand the toughest conditions—whether submerged beneath the waves or scaling rocky cliffs in another zone. With every twist of the red and blue pepsi bezel and every flick of the GMT hand, this watch is a constant reminder that time is not to be wasted—it’s to be conquered.
