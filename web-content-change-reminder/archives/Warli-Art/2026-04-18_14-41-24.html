    Specifications
    Case

    Material: 316L surgical-grade stainless steel

    Diameter: 40 mm

    Lug-to-Lug: 49 mm

    Thickness: 10.3 mm and 12.1 mm with crystal

    Finish: Dual brushed and polished surfaces

    Caseback

    The stainless steel screw-down caseback features an intricately etched peacock, illustrated in authentic Warli art. A tribute to India’s national bird through this timeless folk tradition.

    Crystal

    Domed mineral crystal for classic visual depth and durability

    Dial

    Multi-layered dial, crafted in four segments, made through stamping, etching, and eddi printing inspired from the textures and geometry of Warli Art.

    Hands

    High-polish metallic hour & minute hands and color lacquered chronograph seconds and subdial hands

    Water Resistance

    Up to 50 meters, ready for everyday wear

    Movement

    Seiko VK64 Meca-Quartz hybrid chronograph
